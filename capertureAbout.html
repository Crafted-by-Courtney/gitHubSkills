About

Hi there! I'm Stefon.


I am an artist currently residing in Detroit, Michigan and I am passionate about capturing striking images from my point of view.

My agenda is to inspire all that view my images to feel something moving, whether that be peace, joy, intimacy, grandure, love, or simply admiration.

My work emphasizes the essence of both rural and urban landscapes as well as the power of the human experience.

Please consider checking out my instagram account @Caperture_pix for my most recent work.

Thank you for stopping by and taking a moment to appreciate my art.
